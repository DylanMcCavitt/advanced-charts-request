Boundary

This prototype is a visual review workspace. It produces inspectable chart artifacts only; it does not choose symbols, score outcomes, manage accounts, or perform transactions.

Chart Review Lab uses Alpaca Market Data through server-side endpoints and the open-source Lightweight Charts renderer by TradingView. Advanced Charts access remains pending. See the evidence handoff for public artifact links and page-by-page proof points.
